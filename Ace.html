Ace, his backstory, and why he is Top 3 for favorite siege operators

Ace and what he offers as a operator

Ace is an attacker operator in Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege who specializes in breaching reinforced surfaces. He is known for his S.E.L.M.A. (Selective Explosive Line Charge), a unique gadget that allows him to safely create openings in reinforced walls, hatches, and barricades from a distance. His primary weapons consist of the AK-12 and the M1014 with his secondary weapon being the P9, and his utility being Breach Charges and Claymores. Ace is mainly used to hard breach into the bomb site to eliminate the enemy team or plant the defuser to win the round.

Tech-Savvy and Strategic: He's not only physically adept but also well-versed in using cutting-edge technology to enhance his effectiveness on the field. So far in Rainbow Six Siege, Ace has been portrayed as a dependable and strategic operator, using his specialized gadget to open up defensive positions and shift the balance in favor of his team.
